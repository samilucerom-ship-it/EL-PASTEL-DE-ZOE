# **App Name**: Zoe's Sweet Delights

## Core Features:

- Product Showcase: Display high-quality images of your 9+ cakes with descriptions, prices, and customer reviews to entice customers and highlight the variety.
- WhatsApp Contact Button: A prominent WhatsApp button on each product page, enabling customers to quickly inquire and place orders via WhatsApp using the phone number 928 175 368.
- Social Media Integration: Link to your Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok accounts (@EL_PASTEL_DE_ZOE) to build a strong online presence and encourage engagement across multiple platforms.
- AI-Powered Recommendation Tool: An AI powered recommendation tool that suggests cakes based on user preferences such as dietary restrictions, favorite flavors, and occasions. This helps the user choose which cake to pick from Zoe's cake selection.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Warm pastel pink (#FDBDBD) to evoke feelings of sweetness and delight.
- Background color: Soft off-white (#F5F5F5) for a clean, inviting backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted gold (#DAC0A3) for highlights, buttons, and important information.
- Headline font: 'Belleza' for an elegant and fashionable feel.
- Body font: 'Alegreya' serif for readable product descriptions.
- Custom-designed icons related to cakes and desserts.
- Clean, grid-based layout to showcase the cakes attractively.
